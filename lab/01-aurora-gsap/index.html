(03) Manifesto

We believe a website is not a page. It is a place. A place has weather, gravity, a pulse. So we obsess over the half-second between click and response, the way type breathes when a viewport narrows, the light a color leaves behind after you scroll past it. Most of the internet is built to be skimmed. Ours is built to be felt. We work slowly, ship deliberately, and measure everything in goosebumps per visit.
